# DeadZone: Toon Survival

An action-packed, fast-paced 3D Twin-Stick Shooter built with Godot Engine. Featuring a vibrant toon art style and a classic 3/4 isometric perspective, this game challenges players to survive against never-ending waves of the undead.

## 📝 About The Game

**DeadZone** combines the intense gameplay loop of traditional horde survival games with modern twin-stick shooter mechanics. In a world overrun by stylized but deadly zombies, your only goal is simple: **Survive as long as you can and chase the highest score.**

Players must navigate the battlefield using keyboard controls (WASD) while aiming and shooting with the mouse. As time passes, the apocalypse grows harsher—normal zombies multiply, and dangerous Boss Zombies will spawn to test your reflexes and strategy.

### 🎮 Core Gameplay & Features

*   **Isometric 3/4 Perspective:** Experience the battlefield from an optimized top-down angled view, providing a perfect balance between tactical awareness and 3D depth.
*   **Endless Horde Survival:** No escape, no surrender. The game challenges you to survive against time, where the difficulty scales dynamically the longer you stay alive.
*   **Intense Combat & Weapon Management:** Engage enemies with responsive shooting mechanics. Keep an eye on your ammunition, utilize the reload system wisely, and switch between different weapons to adapt to the situation.
*   **Loot & Progression System:** Slain zombies drop valuable EXP and loot. Collect them to level up, manage your health (HP), and upgrade your arsenal on the fly to withstand the increasing pressure.
*   **Vibrant Toon Aesthetics:** A stylized, non-realistic visual approach using colorful toon/cel-shaded graphics that deliver a clean, readable, and visually engaging experience.
*   **Elite & Boss Variants:** Watch out! Apart from the common shamblers, powerful Super Zombies and massive Bosses will breach the arena with unique behaviors and high health pools.

### 🕹️ How to Play

*   **Move:** `W` `A` `S` `D`
*   **Aim:** Move Mouse Cursor
*   **Shoot:** `Left Click`
*   **Reload:** `R`
*   **Switch Weapon:** `Q` / `E` or `Scroll Wheel`
